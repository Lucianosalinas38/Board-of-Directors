Vault 414

Attention, Vault Dwellers! As you emerge from your long slumber, let the light of a new era shine upon your faces. You stand now at the threshold of a post-apocalyptic world, a world shattered by the ravages of nuclear devastation. But fear not, for within the safe confines of Vault 414, a beacon of hope and resilience, a new chapter in human history is about to unfold. Today, I stand before you to announce the esteemed individuals who will guide our Vault into the uncertain future. They are the guardians of our shared destiny, the stalwarts who will shape our lives within these hallowed walls. Behold, the Board of Directors, chosen with utmost care and dedication to ensure our survival and harmony in this shattered world.


Chairman Of The Board

The Chairman of the Board, Joe Rogan, is a charismatic and influential figure within the Vault community. As the leader of the Vault group, his role is to inspire and guide the residents towards a harmonious and thriving post-cryotherapy life. With his persuasive communication skills and ability to engage diverse perspectives, Rogan ensures that the Vault's policies and decisions are made with the best interests of the community in mind.


Visionary

Elon Musk, the Visionary of the Vault group, is renowned for his forward-thinking ideas and innovative mindset. With a keen eye for technological advancements and a deep understanding of sustainable living, Musk envisions a future within the Vault that embraces cutting-edge technologies and promotes self-sufficiency. His ability to anticipate change and adapt to new circumstances makes him an essential member of the board, guiding the community towards a prosperous future.


Sista Of Support

David Goggins, the Sista of Support in the Vault group, is a formidable individual known for his unwavering determination and resilience. As the resident motivator and mentor, Goggins provides unwavering support to the community members. Whether they face challenging circumstances or need encouragement during their journey, Goggins is there to inspire them to surpass their limits and strive for excellence, ensuring that the Vault's residents remain steadfast and united.


Must Have

Neil deGrasse Tyson, the Must Have director in the Vault group, is a brilliant astrophysicist and educator. His invaluable knowledge of science and astrophysics ensures that the Vault community remains well-informed and intellectually stimulated. Tyson's presence on the board guarantees that scientific advancements, space exploration, and cosmic knowledge are integrated into the educational programs within the Vault, providing a holistic and enlightening experience for all residents.


Nice To Have

Lex Fridman, the Nice to Have director in the Vault group, is a respected AI researcher and technologist. His expertise in artificial intelligence and emerging technologies adds a futuristic and innovative dimension to the board's discussions. Fridman's insights on automation, robotics, and ethical considerations help shape the Vault's technological development and ensure that the community embraces progress while maintaining a balance with human values and well-being.


Must Not Have

The Must Not Have position in the Vault group is occupied by the Tasmanian Devil, a figure representing qualities that are detrimental to the community's harmony and well-being. This individual often displays a confrontational attitude, challenges ethical norms, and disrupts consensus among the board members. Excluding such a person from the board ensures a cohesive decision-making process and a positive environment within the Vault, safeguarding the collective interests and ensuring the peaceful coexistence of its residents.
